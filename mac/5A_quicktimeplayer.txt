##Quicktime Player

#Trim start or end

Edit > Trim > use mouse to pull in from start and/or end

#Trim in the middle

Pause video at the point you want to trim >
Edit > Split Up > use mouse to trim from that point

> Done > View > Show Clips > select one > click Delete on keyboard to remove it

can also Trim a selected Clip in the middle of a Split Clip operation

# Concatenate Clips

Edit > Add Clip To End
