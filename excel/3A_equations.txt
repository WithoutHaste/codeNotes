##Equations

Refers to one cell. If you copy/paste (or square-drag) this equation, the value will be updated to maintain the same relationship between the equation-cell and the referenced-cell as the original has.
<code>
=A0
</code>

A coordinate prefixed with $ will remain static when copy/pasted (or square-dragged).
<code>
=$A0
=A$0
=$A$0
</code>
